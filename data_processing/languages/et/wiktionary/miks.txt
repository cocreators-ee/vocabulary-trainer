Etymology 1

An archaic translative singular form of
mis
(
“
what
”
)

(the modern form is
milleks

). Compare
Finnish
miksi

,
Ingrian

Adverb
miks
(
not comparable
)
why
Interrogative.
Miks
see küll nii on, et alati peab keegi kannatama?
―
Why
is it that someone always has to suffer?
Relative, after a comma.
Peeter teab,
miks
maakera pöörleb.
―
Peeter knows
why
the Earth spins.
In rhetoric questions, expressing regret, reproach or repentance.
Miks
ma küll pidin nii ütlema!
―
Why
did I have to say that!
(
with a negative pronoun or verb
)
Expressing acquiescence, agreement, possibility.
Kas sa mind mäletad? –
Miks
ei mäleta.
―
Do you remember me? – Of course I do.
(literally, “
Why don't [I] remember?
”)
(
with the verb
teadma
(
“
to know
”
)
)
Expressing the ambiguity of a situation.
Jooksis kes teab
miks
minema.
―
(S)he ran away for whatever reason.
(literally, “
[
…
]
who knows
why
.
”)
