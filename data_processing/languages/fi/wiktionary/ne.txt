Inflection

The case suffixes are mostly regular (except the inessive and elative singular). Abessive is never used in the singular and extremely seldom in the plural. Instructive
niin

is more or less a theoretical construction, since it has developed into an adverb, and its current meaning cannot be derived from
ne

.
