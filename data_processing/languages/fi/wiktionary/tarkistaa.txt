Usage notes
tarkastaa
and
tarkistaa
are very close to each other in their meaning. However,
tarkastaa
means "to check, inspect, look at, study" and "to study the suitability, competence or qualifications of something, e.g. in order to find possible defects", whereas
tarkistaa
means "to check, inspect, look" and "to study"
and at the same time
"to correct or amend the possible flaws or defects".
