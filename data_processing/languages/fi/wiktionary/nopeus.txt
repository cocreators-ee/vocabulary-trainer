Usage notes

In technical usage, such as in physics,
nopeus

is a vector corresponding to the English term
velocity

, while
vauhti

is a scalar quantity corresponding to the English term
speed

. In common language, the terms are used synonymously, but are not always interchangeable (e.g. in compounds). Generally speaking,
vauhti

is somewhat less formal.
vauhti

can also be used informally to refer to momentum.
