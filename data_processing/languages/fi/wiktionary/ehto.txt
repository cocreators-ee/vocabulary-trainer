Etymology

Probably
eht-

+‎
-o

, from the same root as
ehtiä

, but here with the meaning "to be able to, to be possible". The word likely originally meant "ability, possibility". Samic words like
Northern Sami
eaktu

are probably borrowed from Finnic.
