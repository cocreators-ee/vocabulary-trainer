)

Near-synonym:

Usage notes

The word
seuraava

is not often used in the sense "the next instance following the present moment", as in "next Sunday" or "until next time"; in such senses, the preferred word is
ensi

.
seuraava

is generally preferred over
ensi

for concrete things:
seuraava potilas
(
“
next patient
”
)

;
Suomen seuraava presidentti
(
“
the next President of Finland
”
)

.
