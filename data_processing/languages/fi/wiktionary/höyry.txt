Usage notes
höyry

for "steam, water vapor/vapour" is a nonspecific term, and
vesihöyry

tends to be used instead in technical contexts (although
höyry

may also be used when there is no risk of ambiguity, e.g. when
vesihöyry

was used earlier, or in compounds).
