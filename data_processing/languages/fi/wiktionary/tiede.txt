Noun
tiede
science
(
collective discipline of learning, including the liberal arts or humanities
)
Usage notes
tiede
in the singular is either a generic term or refers to a particular discipline; the plural
tieteet
may be used collectively of the sciences, including the liberal arts and humanities.
Unlike English
science
in current usage, this term includes more than just the natural sciences. If one wants to refer to them in particular,
luonnontiede
can be used.
