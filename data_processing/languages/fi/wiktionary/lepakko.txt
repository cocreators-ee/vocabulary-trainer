Etymology 2

In older slang, the term was more generally used of an older woman; its current meaning has probably been influenced by
lesbo

(which starts with the same syllable). Originally the same word as
lepakko

(etymology 1); compare the use of
English
bat

as a derogatory term for an old woman.
