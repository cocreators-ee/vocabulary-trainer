Usage notes
(
written
)
:
Kirjoitettu
and
kirjallinen
are pretty close in meaning and both are translated as "written" in English.
Kirjoitettu
is chosen when the focus is in the process of writing in general, and
kirjallinen
refers more to the result of writing.
