Usage notes

The difference between
mahdollisuus

and
tilaisuus

is sometimes subtle, but
mahdollisuus

tends to be used when there is considerable uncertainty about the outcome or when there are several equally good options to choose from, whereas
tilaisuus

is a clearer opportunity to achieve or obtain something desirable which is likely to be missed if not taken.
