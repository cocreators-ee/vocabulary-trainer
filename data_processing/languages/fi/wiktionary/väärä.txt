Usage notes
The superlatives
oikein
and
väärin
are rarely appropriate, because right and wrong are often understood as absolutes, and because of the homonymy with much more frequently used adverbs. People tend to circumvent them by saying
eniten väärä
,
lähinnä oikeaa
and
eniten oikea
, choosing another adjective if available (e.g.
kiero
,
oikeanpuoleinen
,
oikeellinen
) or using
kaikista
as a determiner.
