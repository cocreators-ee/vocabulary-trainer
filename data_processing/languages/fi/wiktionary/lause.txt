Usage notes
In common language, the grammar terms
lause
(
“
clause
”
)
and
virke
(
“
sentence
”
)
are happily confused so that the word

may refer to either of the two; consequently the word

is used relatively seldom. In strict grammatical usage, however, the two terms are distinguished.
