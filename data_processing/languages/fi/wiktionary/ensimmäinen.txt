Etymology

From
Proto-Finnic
*encimeinen

. Derived from the superlative of
ensi

(with the superlative ending
-in

:
*ensin

, genitive
*ensim(m)än

) with +‎
-inen

. The geminated -
mm

- is due to the word falling in line with other superlative words in Finnish (see
-in

) which have been influenced by the comparative suffix
-mpi

. Compare
Estonian
esimene

,
Ingrian

Karelian

,
Livonian

,

eńžimaińe

,

ezmäne

Votic
esimein

.
