Usage notes
The adessive singular form is particularly used when talking about what happened during some week:
sillä
viikolla
―
(during) that
week
,
viime
viikolla
―
(during) last
week
. The essive case is sometimes used in this sense when
viikko
is used with a qualifier or determiner, but not with the most common ones, only adjectives, numerals or e.g. nouns in the genitive case (e.g.
kahtena viikko
na
(
“
on two weeks
”
)
, but not
×tänä viikkona
).
