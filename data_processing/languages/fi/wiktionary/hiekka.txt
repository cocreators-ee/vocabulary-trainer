Etymology

Derived from
hieta

, either as
hieta

+‎
-ikka

(<
*hiedikka

), or as a blend of
hieta

+‎
hiukka

; may be influenced by
hiukka

in any case. Originally primarily an Eastern Finnish word; considered Savonian by Juslenius (1745). Cognate with
Karelian
hiekka

and
Livvi
hiekku

.
