Usage notes
jalka

refers variously to the leg, foot or both. If context is required (such as in anatomy),
jalkaterä

is used for "foot" and
jalka

(or less commonly
jalkavarsi

) for "leg".
Declension

Unlike with most other nouns, an instructive singular form exists.
