Usage notes

The difference between
täällä

and
tässä

depends on the context. Usually
tässä

implies a more precise or exact location than
täällä

. In some other cases,
tässä

is used when describing a location in which the speaker and listener are nearby, and
täällä

is used when the listener is far away.
