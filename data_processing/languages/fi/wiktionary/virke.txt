Usage notes
virke
means
only
the whole sentence or set of clauses ending with a period, exclamation point or question mark. Thus, "
Minä menen kouluun, vaikka en jaksaisi.
" is a
virke
, while "
Minä menen kouluun
" is a
lause

(or more exactly a

).
